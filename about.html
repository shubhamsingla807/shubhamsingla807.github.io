Who am I?


Hi there, I’m a Product Manager based in Bangalore who believes in building impactful experiences that empower people and I inform decisions by data.


Currently working as a Product Manager at STAGE. Previously worked at @Justdial, @Scaler, @PowerPlay, @InterviewBit, @SpektraSystems, @GirlScript and with various clients across India.

Mini CEO :p

Why Product Management?


Because I hate watching good ideas die in meetings.


I’ve always been drawn to building things. In college, I was the kid running the tech fest, debugging logistics one hour and pitching ideas the next. Everything I loved about that, coordinating people, making decisions, shipping, is just PM work in a different costume.


PM lets me live between three worlds. UX, tech, and growth. I can prototype on Monday, pull cohort data on Tuesday, and pitch strategy on Wednesday. Nothing else gives you that range.


And the honest answer? I like building things that real people actually use. Products have an audience. Presentations don’t.

Definitely not me :p

Where do I see myself in future?


Owning a jet ski on a Goa beach... still kidding.


I see myself as an AI-native builder. Someone who makes things, breaks things, ships anyway. I don’t wait for engineering bandwidth to test an idea, and I treat AI as a teammate, not a demo.


Long-term, I want to own a product end-to-end, where I can shape the experiments, the voice, and the experience. Product is the closest thing to making art that also makes money.
